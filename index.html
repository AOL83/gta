Aurora One Ops Hub v0.1

This is the merged first prototype: outer HTML control nest plus a playable 3D operations hub. It keeps the console shell idea and restores the usable movement core.

Control Nest

Passport, inventory, forge, dialogue, save vault, and world launcher live in the outer HTML shell.

Playable Hub

Walk around a futuristic operations yard, enter a vehicle, drive, exit, talk to NPCs, fight drones, collect loot, and forge an upgrade.

Controls

WASD move or drive. Mouse drag look. E interact. 1 attack. F forge or talk. Shift sprint or boost.
